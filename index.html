Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Our Promise

CleanCore is built on a simple principle: your photos never leave your device. We mean this literally.

What We Collect

Nothing.

Our App Store Privacy Label declares "Data Not Collected". We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information, including:

  • Photos, videos, or any media content
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage patterns or behavior data
  • Location data
  • Contact information (unless you email us directly)

How We Process Data

All photo analysis happens 100% on your device using Apple's native frameworks:

  • PhotoKit: Accesses your photo library
  • Vision: Analyzes image similarity and quality
  • StoreKit: Handles in-app purchases

We use zero third-party SDKs. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising networks.

Network Activity

CleanCore makes zero network requests. You can verify this yourself:

  1. Open iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → App Privacy Report
  2. Look for "CleanCore" in the Network Activity section
  3. You should see zero network requests

Data Storage

The app stores only:

  • Usage quota data: Your monthly free-tier operation count (stored locally in SwiftData)
  • Purchase receipts: Managed by Apple's StoreKit (we never see your payment info)

All data stays on your device and in your iCloud (if you enable it in iOS Settings).

iCloud

If you enable iCloud for CleanCore in iOS Settings, your quota data may sync across your devices via Apple's CloudKit. This is optional and controlled by you through iOS Settings. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Children's Privacy

CleanCore does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect zero data, the app is safe for all ages.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to access, modify, or delete on our end. All app data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
